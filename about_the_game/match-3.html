Match-3



About the game

This is single player game.
This is very similar to Candy Crush Saga or Bejeweled this is a game where in simple terms make a match of three or more. A match is the same colour in a row or in a column.

How to play?

  • At the start choose start by pressing enter.
  • You are allowed to swap two colours in a manner that they create a match.
  • The goal of the game is to create as many Three in a Rows or Three in a Columns so that you can beat the required score given on the left within 60 seconds.
  • You will have unlimited moves.
  • For every colour that is destroyed when you make a match, you will get one second more to think.
  • Press 'p' key to Pause or Play the game.
  • You are all set! All the Best!
  • Try to reach the level 30 within 15 minutes of playing the game.
  • That will make you can expert in the game
