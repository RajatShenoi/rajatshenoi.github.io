Pong



About the game

This is two player game with a fairly simple goal.
In the game there exists a ball and two paddles (the rectangular structures on the left and right). Each player get's a change to serve and each player controls the paddle on their side trying to avoid the ball from hitting the boundary on their side which would result in a point of the opponent. The first to score 10 points wins.

How to play?

  • To move the left paddle upwards use the 'w' key
  • To move the left paddle downwards use the 's' key
  • To move the right paddle upwards use the up arrow key
  • To move the right paddle upwards use the down arrow key
  • First player to score 10 points wins
