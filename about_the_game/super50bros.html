Super 50 Bros



About the game

This is single player game.
This is similar to Nintendo's Mario where there is a player whom we can control and allow it to move front, back and jump up. The goal of the game is to avoid enemies who try to kill you and reach the end of the level where a flag appears to move on to the next level. While doing so, you will find a key which you must use to unlock a lock and only once it has been unlocked, a flag will appear at the end of the level on the flag pole, else there will exist only a flag pole without a flag. Touch the flag after unlocking all the locked blocks to move on to the next level.

How to play?

  • Once you have opened the game, press enter to start.
  • You will find a green alien which you can control.
  • Use the right arrow to move to the right.
  • Use the left arrow to move to the left.
  • Use the space bar to jump.
  • As you move jump through the gaps in the ground.
  • You will find block in the sky as you move through the game.
  • You can jump on top of them or jump from below therefore colliding the head of the alien against the block.
  • This can cause a spawning of a gem as shown in the image as if you collect that, your score will increase.
  • Next, you will also find snails, which if touched will kill you.
  • Therefore, you must either avoid them or jump on top of them which will kill them.
  • You then have to find for a key which will be available in the game and pick it.
  • Then find the locked block and collide with it once you have picked the key, now the block is unlocked, shown by the dissapearing of the block.
  • This will spawn a flag at the end of the game which you have to touch to move to the next level.
  • That's it! All the best!
  • Your goal must be to reach level 20 which is a reasonable fair challenge for you to complete soon enough.
