Legend Of 50



About the game

This is single player game.
In this game, you are in control of a character against which many other enemies come to attach and kill. You in return must attack them and kill them, increasing your score and level. You must kill all the enemies in a room after which you click the button which opens the doors on the four sides and go through any of them to the next level which will be more difficult than the previous one.

How to play?

  • Press enter to start
  • Press up arrow to move up.
  • Press down arrow to move down.
  • Press right arrow to move right.
  • Press left arrow to move left.
  • Press space bar to attack with your sword.
  • Kill your enemies without getting too close to them.
  • In rare and lucky scenarios, you will get lives from vanquised enemies which will increase your health.
  • Kill all of them and then stand on top on the red button which will open up the doors on all four sides and go through one of them to the next harder than previous level
