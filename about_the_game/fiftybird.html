Fifty Bird



About the game

This is single player game.
In this game there is a bird which you must protect. As the game goes on there will be obstacles through which you must pass (from in between the obstacles as shown in the image.) Everytime you pass a bamboo or simply a pillar, you get one point.

How to play?

  • At the start choose between the two levels
  • Press '1' for an easy game.
  • Press '2' for a hard game.
  • Press space bar to jump up.
  • Press 'p' to pause or play.
  • Continue indefinetly.
  • A reasonable score in easy will be 30 and then you may attempt the hard version of the game.
